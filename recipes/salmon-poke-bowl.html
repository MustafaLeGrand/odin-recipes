Salmon Poke Bowl

Description: Healthy & easy to prepare, the perfect selection of high protein and carbs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound of sushi-grade salmon
  • 2 tablespoons of fermented tamari or coconut aminos
  • 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon of coconut vinegar
  • 1 glove of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 of teaspoon raw honey

Toppings

  • Avocado cut in cubes
  • cucumber, cut into small cubes
  • Shredded Carrots
  • Riced Cauliflower
  • Green Onion, chopped
  • Mango, chopped

Steps:

  1. Cut the fish into small bite-size cubes and placer them in a shallow bowl.
  2. Whisk the tamari, sesame oil, coconut vinegar, garlic and honey in a small bowl and pour over the fish.
  3. Toss the fish gently so the marinade is incorporated.
  4. Cover and place in the fridge to marinate for 30 minutes.
  5. After the fish has marinated, serve with the toppings of your choice.
  6. Decorate with your toppings.
